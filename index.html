About Me

Hello there, my name is Zach Remer, I‘m a passionate student in computer program development. Graduating with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the University of Colorado, I possess a solid foundation in coding and problem-solving, showcasing proficiency in languages such as Python and Java.

My journey into the tech realm began with an internship at CORE Electric Cooperative, where I honed my IT desktop support skills, catering to the needs of a vast workforce both in person and remotely. This experience not only fortified my technical acumen but also instilled in me the value of collaboration within a team environment. Going beyond traditional computer science, I ventured into the realm of customer-centric roles, serving as an Expo and Shucker at Jax Fish House and later assuming the roles of Cashier and Bartender at Torchy Tacos. These experiences polished my communication skills and fostered a deep understanding of customer needs. My career and college highlights reflect my commitment to innovation and continuous learning. From developing an alert system in SharePoint to creating interactive games and integrating APIs, I have consistently sought to push boundaries and explore new horizons in software development.

With a diverse skill set encompassing coding languages, software programs, and IT support expertise, I am able to make meaningful contributions to cutting-edge projects. Eager to embark on new challenges and chart new territories in the ever-evolving landscape of technology, I am driven by my passion for innovation and a dedication to excellence.
